\subsubsection*{Critical Numbers and the Closed Interval Method}

\begin{theorem}[Fermat's Theorem]
    If \(f\) has a local maximum or minimum at \(c\), and if \(f'(c)\) exists,
    then \(f'(c)=0\).
\end{theorem}
