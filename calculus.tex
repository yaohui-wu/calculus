\section*{Introduction}
\textbf{Calculus} is the mathematical study of continuous change established by
\textbf{Issac Newton} and \textbf{Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz} in the 17th
century.
\textbf{Single variable calculus} stuides \textbf{derivatives} and
\textbf{integrals} of functions of one variable and their relationship stated
by the \textbf{fundamental theorem of calculus}.
\[\int_a^b f(x)dx = F(b)-F(a)\]
